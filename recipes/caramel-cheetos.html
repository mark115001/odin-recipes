Caramel Cheetos Recipe

These caramel cheetos are the perfect salty-sweet snack. They are great for any TV watching or game day.


Ingredients:

  • 2 (8.5 oz) bags crunchy cheese curls (such as Cheetos)
  • 1 cup light corn syrup
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 stick (8oz) butter

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C). Line large
    rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper

  2. Pour cheetos into a large bowl

  3. Combine light corn syruup, brown sugar and butter in a saucepan over medium
    high heat. Stir constantly and bring the mixture to a boil. Cook for 1 minute and
    then pour mixture over the cheetos

  4. Working quickly, stir to coat cheetos with sugar mixture and pour them onto
    the prepared baking sheet. Spread them out into and even layer.

  5. Bake in the preheated over for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. Remove from
    the oven and allow to cool completely. Break any clumps into small pieces.
    Serve immediately or store in an airtight container.
