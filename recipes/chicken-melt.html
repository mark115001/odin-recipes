Kimchi Chicken Melt Sandwich Recipe

This kimchi chicken melt sandwich feels a little extraâ€”these flavors will bring your taste buds alive.
There's lot's of cheese, using both provolone and havarti, and inside the melted cheese is a simple mixture
of precooked shredded chicken, kimchi, and mayonnaise.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 Cup of sheredded cooked chicken
  • 2 tablespoons kimchi, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon softened butter
  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 2 slices of provoline cheese
  • 2 slices of havarti cheese

Directions:

  1. In a small bowl combine sherededed chicken, kimchi, and mayonnaise

  2. Preheat a small skillet over medium heat. Spread 1/2 tablespoon butter on one
    side of a slice of bread. Place 1 slice bread butter-side-down in skillet; place 1
    slice of provoline and 1 slice of Havarti cheese onto bread. Top with chicken mixture,
    then with remaining slice provoline and remaining Havarti. Butter second
    bread slice with remaining butter on once side and place butter-side-up o top of sandwich

  3. Cook sandwich in a skillet until bread is lightly bowned and flip over, about 2 1/2
    on each side

  4. Enjoy and leave a review with your thoughts.
